Welcome to Louisville's most active board gaming group! Card Board Gamers started with the vision of providing opportunities to play two types of gaming that didn't have a public venue in Louisville: Living Card Games (LCGs) and long epic thematic games such as Arkham Horror. After several years, we continue to support those types of games as well as virtually any type of tabletop gaming as we have changed and adapted to our growing membership. Grab some dice or cards and join us for some great gaming! We regularly schedule gaming days. Members are encouraged to bring whatever hobby games they enjoy and try games brought by others.
